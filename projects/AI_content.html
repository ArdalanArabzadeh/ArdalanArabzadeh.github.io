AI-Generated Visual Content

Project Overview

This project presents selected AI-generated video works conceived, directed, and assembled end-to-end by me. Each piece combines concept development, prompt engineering, visual consistency control, and post-production into a coherent creative outcome.

The focus is not on individual tools, but on how generative systems are orchestrated to produce intentional, commercially relevant, and narratively controlled visuals.

Project Details

  • Type: AI-Generated Visual & Cinematic Content
  • Tools: AI video & image generation, AI voice synthesis, professional video editing

Tools & Approach

Depending on the project, a selective mix of AI video and image generation tools (e.g. Grok, Veo, Sora, Qwen, Banana), AI voice synthesis (Google TTS), and professional editing and compositing tools (CapCut) was used.

Tooling evolves rapidly; the emphasis here is on creative direction, narrative control, and consistency rather than on any single platform.

Video 1 — AI Persona Film: Human, Amplified

An AI-generated on-camera persona based on my real appearance, placed across controlled environments. The film explores identity, agency, and authorship — positioning the human as the constant amid accelerating systems.

The focus is on identity-preserving character generation, believable performance, dialogue timing, and cinematic direction suitable for personal branding or thought-leadership content.

Video 2 — ARDALAN / CORE (Fictional Product Commercial)

A high-end commercial for a non-existent product: ARDALAN / CORE, a screenless personal AI hub. The film treats the product as a real consumer technology through restrained, premium visual language.

This piece demonstrates commercial storytelling with generative media — product visualization, brand consistency, abstraction, and pacing without over-explanation.

Video 3 — Artificial Shiraz: A City That Breathes

A cinematic interpretation of Shiraz as a living city. The work integrates real landmarks and urban elements into an abstract, non-touristic narrative that cannot be credibly stock-sourced or replicated.

The focus lies on atmosphere design, scene continuity, place-specific identity, and cinematic pacing using AI as a storytelling medium.

Closing Note

These works are intentionally short, precise, and concept-driven. They reflect how I think, direct, and build with AI — not as a novelty, but as a creative and engineering medium.
